PRODUCT ENGINEERING

Enterprise Test Management is changing fast with Agile and DevOps. The need of the hour is to employ test management methodologies which can ensure quality and give assurance to stakeholders however at the same time work across technical teams and liaise with business. The boundaries between test team and development team is coming down and test management needs to have an overarching effect across the teams. We at Atalgo have spearheaded this change from the front. Our test management methodology has evolved from solving the practical challenges in various enterprises adopting new software development methodologies while ensuring the quality. We have a proprietary methodology catering for multi-vendor and complex digital transformation projects and ensures quality while proactively addressing business traceability, risk management, stakeholder management and change management. Atalgo Computing has delivered successful test management projects for enterprises.
