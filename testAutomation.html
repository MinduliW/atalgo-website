TEST AUTOMATION

The scope, technique as well as value add of test automation is fast increasing. In traditional IT projects, testing was done in a silo and independently which was costly and not always best way to ensure the quality. With the advent of DevOps, Continuous Testing, Agile and Shift Left paradigms, testing has become more pervasive and tools dependent. We can not achieve the desired level of continuous quality without a robust automation strategy and framework.

Atalgo has years of experience in designing the test automation strategy as well as frameworks for enterprise environments. We have successfully proven our capabilities by driving business value in test automation in public sector, transport, superannuation and financial industry. We have our proprietary test automation methodology of IMO (Initial, Managed, Optimized) which can enable any IT project to become mature in Continuous Testing in most optimal manner.

Leveraging the years of experience and employing Continuous Testing principles, we have launched our codeless test automation platform, Flame. This platform caters for SME as well and large enterprise projects by enabling the automation engineers develop, execute, and maintain Web, Mobile as well as API automation scripts from same platform.
